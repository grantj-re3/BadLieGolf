# Putting: Reading greens

1. [Golf: Kellie Stenzel | 10 ways to perfect your green-reading — and drain more putts | 2022](https://golf.com/instruction/putting/how-perfect-green-reading-drain-putts/)
   - Includes a putting lesson video with So Yeon Ryu
   - Know that greens are built for drainage | Start reading before
     you arrive on the green | Priority No. 1: distance and speed |
     Study the area around your line | ‘Sectionalize’ longer putts |
     Walk in small circles | Wide stance directly behind the ball |
     Play more break on downhill putts | Dial in your tilt-o-meter |
     Technology

1. [Meandmygolf: Piers Ward & Andy Proudman | RICKIE FOWLER - HOW I READ GREENS (video) | 2020](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PEac_olXeDI)
   - Rickie reads while walking to the green
   - He reads from:
     * the low-side
     * the hole-position to decide where the ball will enter the hole
     * the ball-position to decide speed and line
   - Commits to the read
   - He reads the green using feet and eyes
   - He has been using a line marked on the ball for 6 years; took him 2-3 months to train his eyes
   - He uses a putting mirror before a round to ensure a consistent set up
   - Pace control:
     * Hitting the ball solid: Concentrate on hitting the centre of the clubface
     * Backswing and follow through lengths are similar
