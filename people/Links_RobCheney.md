# Rob Cheney Golf

1. :thumbsup: [Driver Ball Position Mistake That Everyone Gets Wrong! (video) | 2025](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zT2ilWa9WcU)
   - Align ball with lead shoulder & lead heel
   - Bump your pelvis forward (don't move shoulders back)
   - Close your stance a little (perhaps 20°)
   - [The ball will draw if clubface is closed relative to path at impact]
   - The Ball Position With Every Club video [here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=E1DOJTTWIOE)

1. :thumbsup: [This Underrated Move Will Change Your Driver Forever! (video) | 2025](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FFOkbBfBzk8)
   - Complete the backswing: Closed stance | "Feel" a pause at the top
   - **Start of downswing**: Drop/pull your hands down fast | Body remains closed | Feel body turn start late at P6
   - When combined with video below: Dropping the hands occurs at the same time as pelvis sway (while closed & before body turn)
   - Similar video with Rob & Eric Cogorno [here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aZyox3Z9d8c)

1. [The Magic Downswing Move For Perfect Ball Striking (video) | 2025](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=06heY1oCOu4)
   - Move your pelvis forward while closing your hips by 5-10° to the path
   - At the top, your pelvis is about 45° to the path
   - **Start of downswing**: Sway your pelvis towards the target | Body remains closed | Feel body turn start late
   - Synchronise your hip turn with your swing speed
