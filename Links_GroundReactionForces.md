# Ground Reaction Forces (GRF)

## Introduction

The GRF downswing sequence is *lateral*, then *rotational* and finally *vertical*.

1. [The Global Platform for Innovative Technologies | Ground Reaction Force The Engine of the Golf Swing (video) | 2026](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=I-qLxS3prAA)


## Joe Plecker

1. :thumbsup: [The Physics of Power: Unlocking Torque in Your Golf Swing | Titleist Tips (video) | 2025](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=h-K3UMWTJ54)
   - Pulse drill 1: Combines the *sequence of forces on the lead leg* | Hold iron upside down | During the downswing, fall onto the
     ball of your lead foot and brake | Push back towards the heel of the lead foot by stepping back | Stop the downswing at P6
   - Pulse drill 2: The same as pulse drill 1 but: Hold the club the right way around | Make a full swing | Step back as before
1. :thumbsup: [Generate Power in the Golf Swing with Your Lead Leg | Titleist Tips (video) | 2025](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5FTMchSLlfM)
   - Drill 1: During the downswing, lift your lead foot and land (to get the feeling of braking the lateral shift)
   - Lead foot also pushes back away from the ball (rotating the body) and up (vertical force)
   - Then Pulse drills described above
1. [Try This Drill to Stop Early Extending | Titleist Tips (video) | 2025](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NGVwK3DtA-I)
   - Put a 'slider' or paper plate [or plastic icecream container lid) under your trail foot |
     In the downswing at P5, push it firmly into the ground and pull it back
1. [Joe Plecker - How Torque Forces Work in the Golf Swing (video) | 2023](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EjzCrs8HePc&t=32m19s)
   - 32m19s-33m32s: Pulse drill for golfers who move 'heel to toe' in the downswing
