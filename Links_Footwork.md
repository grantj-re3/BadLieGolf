1. [Top Speed Golf: Clay Ballard | Learn Proper Golf Footwork | 2017](https://topspeedgolf.com/vault/learn-proper-golf-footwork/)
   - Drill #1: Slow swing without then with a ball
   - Drill #2: Ditto but only stand on your left leg and swing with just your left arm.
   - Drill #3: Ditto but switch to your right side.
   - Drill #4: Repeat drill #1 as a slow step drill.

   - Drills to fix your footwork: Find neutral at setup | Load the trail foot in the backswing |

1. [Golf Tips Magazine: Chris Johnston | How To Develop Great Golf Footwork | 2018](https://golftipsmag.com/instruction/full-swing/how-to-develop-great-golf-footwork/)
   - Drills using a cinder/breeze/cement block off the back foot: Overhand Throw | Underhand Toss | Transfer that feel into your swing
