# Lee Kopanski

## Introduction

He advocates:

- Good alignment
- Aim left, swing right (and his simply made practice-jig to encourage this)
- Swing fast, softly
- Short game
- Drills

1. [English playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL-lt7SpPliiqDxXpJwozjLyTEkyH7J0ZC)

1. How to construct and use Lee's *practice-jig*
   [here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w5Plxv82MF0) and
   [here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2hACZ7DfjRo)
   - The nearest club points to the target, so set your clubface, feet, knees, hips and shoulders parallel to it
   - The other club gives your downswing path [while the body remains fairly closed]
   - This promotes Lee's *aim left, swing right* "loop"


## Long game

1. [Biggest Swing Killer! (video) | 2021](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eebIr3G6HY8&t=6m53s)
   - Stance and alignment problems (esoecially if you swing over the top)
   - To reprogram/reset your body then you've got to do the complete opposite
   - *Fix: Aim [body and feet] left | Swing to the right*

1. [INCREASE SPEED IN YOUR GOLF SWING! 2 simple ways to dramatically increase club head speed! (video) | 2024](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-oCY5XU7zmM)
   - [1m29s] #1: Swing fast, stop hard (or accelerate to brake): Feel like you finish hard with low hands | Drill: Arm speed without upper body |
     Drill: Keep shoulders parallel with the target line and club shaft finishes on neck | *In the real swing, the momentum of the club
     pulls the upper body through after impact* | *Feel as though shoulders are closed at impact*
   - [8m26s] #2: Swing fast, softly. | *Feel relaxed/floppy: no tension in hands, arms, wrists and shoulders* |
     *Drill: Continuous half swings [while maintaining correct footwork, late body turn and straight arms]*

1. [Grip and Wrist Hinge (video) | 2018](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qjSjIIsZGFo)
   - Good body alignment | Grip the handle across your fingers | *Club shaft should be an extension of your left arm (looking from the front)
     so hands will be near your left pocket* | This will ensure your left wrist is straight/neutral (not cupped or bowed) | Result: Clubface
     will be parallel to left forearm at the top

1. [Clubface & Body Alignment - What Can Go Wrong From Here? (video) | 2018](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AZz_aIMaKPY)
   - On a golf course (or end of driving range), check the alignment of your feet
   - Setup alignment: clubface, feet together then feet apart

1. [Hands First - Body Follows! (video) | 2023](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4OuajLQRgBg)
   - The body reacts to the clubface and follows the hand, arm and wrist movement, and not the other way around

1. [L to L Power! (video) | 2023](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=A2nHKIZvpw8)
   - *The feet together drill and the L-to-L drill work perfectly together* allowing the body to be pulled
     into position by the swinging motion of the hands and arms

1. [More LAG for MASSIVE GAINS in SPEED & POWER! (video) | 2024](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=G4SusaIu4Bs)


## Greenside bunkers

1. :thumbsup: [#1 Bunker Shot Killer (video) | 2022](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IjSKVbojTRE)
   - *This video is pretty good and has pretty complete coverage*
   - Aim left, swing right | Look at the direction of your divot | Speed comes from the wrists | Soft relaxed hands | Practice
   - Exaggerate the feeling of the club head passing the hands through impact | This adds loft and bounce to the shot... making your safety mechanism bigger

1. :thumbsup: [Low Hands and use the Heel for great Bunker Shots! (video) | 2024](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Zlr4aOgYTbQ)
   - [2m48s - 3m28s] ***I feel as though the sharp point of the heel is digging into the sand.
     That's how low the hands should be... and works well on both dry and hard and wet sand.***

1. [Lose the FEAR ! (video) | 2023](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CCihXsCtP24&t=4m59s)
   - Active wrists: hinge, unhinge through impact then rehinge
   - With relaxed arms and wrists and extra soft hands, swing the clubhead
     and allow the swinging motion to gently pull the body with it
   - [15m13s - 15m58s] Tiger's drill
   - Get your hands your arms and your wrists to work independently from your shoulders and your upper body


## Pitching and chipping

Like Dan Grieve, quite a few of his short shots hole-out!

1. :thumbsup: [The Easiest Chip in Golf (video) | 2022](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HG5_HtTsM1c)
   * The shot must be free of tension (see more [here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ot_qkt40F10)) |
     Play the ground | Other points as below

1. [Chipping made easy! (video) | 2018](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3l-AcfeezJQ)
   * Keep your body down through the shot
   * He prefers to use his wrists | Alternatively use no wrists (like a long putting stroke)
   * The heel digs and the toe cuts. There is more bounce on the toe than on the heel.
   * Stand closer to the ball | Make the shaft steeper (which lifts the heel off the ground) | Swing along the line of your feet
